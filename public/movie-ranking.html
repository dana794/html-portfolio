The Best Movies According to Matt

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Forrest Gump

This is one of my favorite movies of all time because i love Tom Hanks as an actor. He does such a good job portraying the character in a realistic and believeable way. I love how they incorporate real life elements into the movie such as Apple, Bubba Gump Shrimp Company, etc. The story that this movie tells is just a timeless classic.

The Fast and the Furious

This has always been one of my favorites, simply because of the cars. I saw this movie for the first time with my dad as a kid and it ignited my love for cars. I went on to own lots of fast cars, all because of the seed that this movie planted.

The Santa Clause

This movie is one of my favorites for nostalgia purposes. I used to watch it as a kid around the holiday's and i loved the sotry that it told. I watch it as an adult now because when i married my wife in 2014, we quickly realized that we both absolutely LOVED the Santa Clause movies. We watch all 3 of them every year around Christmas time. It's become a tradition for us.
